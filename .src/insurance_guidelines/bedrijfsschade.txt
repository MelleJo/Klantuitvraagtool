Guidelines for Bedrijfsschadeverzekering:

1. Explain that this insurance covers the financial consequences if the business is (partially) shut down after a calamity.
2. Mention that fixed costs (such as personnel costs) and net profit can be insured.
3. Discuss the importance of evaluating whether the payout period is still sufficient, given current circumstances like material shortages and longer delivery times.
4. Structure the advice as follows:
   a. Brief explanation of what bedrijfsschadeverzekering covers
   b. Current situation (payout period, insured amount)
   c. Discussion of potential risks (e.g., longer recovery times)
   d. Recommendations
   e. Question for engagement
